🔒 Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 16, 2026

TL;DR: VIRU runs 100% locally on your machine. We don't collect, store, or share any of your data. Ever.

1. Introduction

VIRU ("Virtual Intelligence Response Unit") is a self-hosted, open-source AI coding assistant. This privacy policy explains our commitment to your privacy and how we handle (or rather, don't handle) your data.

2. Data Collection

We collect ZERO data.

VIRU is designed to run entirely on your local machine. This means:

  • Your code never leaves your computer
  • Your conversations with VIRU stay local
  • Your project files remain on your machine
  • No telemetry, analytics, or tracking of any kind

3. How VIRU Works

VIRU uses:

  • Ollama: A local LLM runtime that processes everything on your machine
  • SQLite Database: Stored locally in your VIRU installation directory
  • Docker Volumes: All data persists in local Docker volumes on your system

Unless you explicitly configure VIRU to use OpenAI's API (optional), no data is ever sent to external servers.

4. Website & Landing Page

This website (landing page) uses:

  • Essential Cookies: Only to remember your cookie consent choice (stored in browser localStorage)
  • No Analytics: We don't use Google Analytics, Facebook Pixel, or any tracking scripts
  • No Third-Party Services: No external fonts from Google (self-hosted), no CDNs tracking you

5. Third-Party Services (Optional)

If you choose to configure VIRU with external services:

  • OpenAI API: If you add your API key, your prompts will be sent to OpenAI. Subject to OpenAI's privacy policy.
  • GitHub: Source code is hosted on GitHub. Subject to GitHub's privacy policy.

These are 100% optional and controlled by you.

6. Your Rights

Since we don't collect any data, there's nothing to:

  • Request access to
  • Request deletion of
  • Opt out of
  • Export

You have complete control because everything is on your machine.

7. GDPR Compliance

VIRU is inherently GDPR-compliant because:

  • We don't process any personal data
  • All data stays on your device
  • You are the data controller

8. Children's Privacy

VIRU is a developer tool and not intended for children under 13. Since we don't collect data, this is a non-issue, but we recommend parental guidance.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy to reflect changes in our practices or for legal reasons. Check this page periodically for updates. The "Last Updated" date will change.

10. Open Source Transparency

VIRU is MIT-licensed open source. You can audit the entire codebase at:
https://github.com/rajpratham1/VIRU

If you find any privacy concerns, please open an issue on GitHub.

11. Contact

For privacy questions or concerns:

  • GitHub Issues: VIRU Issues
  • GitHub Discussions: VIRU Discussions
Bottom Line: VIRU is built for privacy-conscious developers. Your code is yours. Your data is yours. We can't access it even if we wanted to.
